We're excited to announce AISwarms, a new capability that enables multiple AI agents to collaborate on complex tasks within your automation flows.

<!-- truncate -->

AISwarms coordinate specialized AI agents that work together to solve complex problems. Each agent in the swarm handles a specific aspect of the task, enabling sophisticated analysis and decision-making.
