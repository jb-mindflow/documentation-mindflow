# Quickstart

Once you have designed your scenario, it's time to build your flow in the platform! Follow this page to discover the 5 key steps to effectively conduct actions within our platform.

## Step 1: Choose a trigger

Every flow starts with a trigger. Select how you want your flow to begin — manually, on a schedule, or via webhook.

## Step 2: Add actions

Add steps to your flow by selecting the services you want to interact with and the operations you want to perform.

## Step 3: Configure conditions

Use conditions to add decision logic to your flow. You can branch your flow based on data from previous steps.

## Step 4: Set up loops

Create loops to iterate over lists of items, such as processing each alert in a batch.

## Step 5: Test and activate

Run your flow in test mode to verify it works correctly, then activate it for production use.
