# Okta

At its core, Okta is a cloud-based Identity and Access Management (IAM) platform. It acts as the secure bridge between your users (whether they are employees, partners, or customers) and the technology they need to use. Instead of manually managing dozens of different credentials, passwords, and access rules across various applications, Okta centralizes identity management into one secure, streamlined hub.

Here is what Okta primarily helps your organization achieve:

-   **Single Sign-On (SSO):** Allows users to log in just once with a single set of credentials to instantly access all of their approved cloud and on-premise applications.

-   **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Adds crucial layers of security—such as biometric scans, authenticator apps, or hardware tokens—to ensure the person logging in is exactly who they say they are.

-   **Automated Lifecycle Management:** Seamlessly provisions (grants) and de-provisions (revokes) software access automatically as people join, move within, or leave your organization.

-   **API Access Management:** Secures your custom APIs, ensuring only authorized applications and developers can interact with your sensitive system data.
