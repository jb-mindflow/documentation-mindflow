# AIBuild: Faster Flow Creation

AIBuild uses AI to accelerate flow creation. Describe what you want to automate and AIBuild generates the flow structure for you.

## How to use AIBuild

Open the flow editor and activate AIBuild. Describe your automation scenario in natural language and AIBuild will generate the flow steps, select appropriate integrations, and configure the logic.

## Reviewing generated flows

Always review AIBuild's output before activating the flow. Check that the correct services and operations are selected and that the logic matches your requirements.

## Iterating with AIBuild

You can refine the generated flow by providing additional instructions to AIBuild or by manually editing individual steps.
