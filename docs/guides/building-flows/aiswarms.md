# AISwarms: Multi-Agent Orchestration

AISwarms enable multiple AI agents to collaborate on complex tasks within a single flow step.

## What are AISwarms?

AISwarms coordinate multiple specialized AI agents that work together to solve complex problems. Each agent in the swarm handles a specific aspect of the task.

## Configuring a swarm

Define the agents in your swarm, their roles, and how they should collaborate. Set the orchestration strategy and the conditions for task completion.

## Use cases

AISwarms excel at complex analysis tasks, multi-perspective reviews, and scenarios requiring different types of expertise applied in sequence or parallel.
