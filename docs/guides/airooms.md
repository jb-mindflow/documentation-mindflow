# AI Rooms: Turning Prompts into Powerful Actions

AI Rooms allow you to interact with Mindflow using natural language. Describe what you want to automate and let AI build and execute the flow for you.

## How it works

Enter a prompt describing the automation you need. AI Rooms analyzes your request, selects the appropriate integrations, and constructs a flow to accomplish your goal.

## Use cases

AI Rooms is ideal for ad-hoc tasks, quick automations, and exploring what's possible with your connected services — all without manually building a flow.

## Best practices

Be specific in your prompts, mention the services you want to use, and describe the expected outcome clearly for best results.
