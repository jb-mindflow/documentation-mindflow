# Import a Flow from Our Template Library

Get started quickly by importing pre-built flow templates from the Mindflow template library.

## Browsing templates

Access the template library from the Mindflow dashboard. Browse by category, use case, or integration to find templates that match your needs.

## Importing a template

Click on a template to preview its structure and description. When you find one you like, import it into your workspace to customize and activate.

## Customizing imported flows

After importing, customize the flow to match your specific requirements — update integration credentials, modify conditions, and adjust step parameters.
