# Monitoring Executions

Track the status of your flows, troubleshoot failures, and analyze execution history.

## Execution dashboard

The execution dashboard provides a real-time view of all flow runs across your organization. Filter by status, flow name, or time range to find specific executions.

## Execution details

Click on any execution to see step-by-step details, including input/output data, timing, and any errors that occurred.

## Troubleshooting

When a flow fails, the execution details page highlights the failing step and provides error information to help you diagnose and fix the issue.

## Notifications

Configure notifications to alert your team when flows fail or when specific conditions are met during execution.
