# Administrating the Platform

Manage your Mindflow organization, including users, permissions, and platform settings.

## User management

Add and manage team members, assign roles, and control access to flows and integrations.

## Roles and permissions

Mindflow provides role-based access control to ensure team members only have access to the resources they need.

## Organization settings

Configure organization-wide settings including SSO, API keys, and notification preferences.

## Audit logs

Review audit logs to track changes made to flows, integrations, and organization settings.
