# Training

Get up to speed with Mindflow through our structured training program.

## Platform walkthrough

Familiarize yourself with the Mindflow interface, including the flow editor, dashboard, and integration management areas.

## Hands-on exercises

Follow guided exercises to build increasingly complex flows, starting from simple service-to-service automations and progressing to multi-step orchestrations with conditions and loops.

## Certification

Complete the training program to earn your Mindflow certification, demonstrating your proficiency with the platform.
